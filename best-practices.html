Best Practices

Discover tips and best practices for getting the most out of OurApp and building a robust workflow.

Security Best Practices
- Use strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication where possible.
- Regularly back up your data.
Performance Best Practices
- Monitor usage and optimize based on analytics.
- Automate deployments to reduce manual errors.
- Review logs frequently for any issues.
