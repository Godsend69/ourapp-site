Install OurApp on Your Device

Get an app-like experience — right from your browser.

How to add OurApp to your Home Screen:

Android (Chrome, Edge, Brave, etc.)

  • Open godsend69.github.io/ourapp-site in your browser.
  • Tap the Menu (⋮ or ☰) in the top-right.
  • Select Add to Home screen or Install App.
  • Confirm — OurApp will appear on your home screen like a regular app.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  • Open godsend69.github.io/ourapp-site in Safari.
  • Tap the Share button (↑).
  • Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  • Tap Add — the OurApp icon will appear on your home screen.
Tip: Using OurApp this way gives you instant access, full-screen mode, and a true app feel — without any downloads.
